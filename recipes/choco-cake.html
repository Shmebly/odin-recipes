Chocolate Cake

Description

This delicious chocolate cake recipe is going to knock your socks off. This cake is based on the Old Fashioned Hershey's recipe. This recipe will be having you go for second and even thirds! Be warned though, if you aren't a fan of chocolate this recipe isn't for you!

Ingredients

for this cake you'll need the following ingredients.

  • all-purpose flour
  • sugar
  • unsweetened cocoa powder
  • baking powder
  • salt
  • espresso powder
  • milk- you can also use buttermilk, almond milk, oat milk, or coconut milk
  • oil- you can use vegetable, canola, or melted coconut oil
  • eggs- when baking, it's good to use room temperature eggs
  • vanilla extract
  • boiling water
