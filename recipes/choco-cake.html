Chocolate Cake

Description

This delicious chocolate cake recipe is going to knock your socks off. This cake is based on the Old Fashioned Hershey's recipe. This recipe will be having you go for second and even thirds! Be warned though, if you aren't a fan of chocolate this recipe isn't for you!

Ingredients

for this cake you'll need the following ingredients.

  • all-purpose flour

  • sugar

  • unsweetened cocoa powder

  • baking powder

  • salt

  • espresso powder- really makes the cake

  • milk- you can also use buttermilk, almond milk, oat milk, or coconut milk

  • oil- you can use vegetable, canola, or melted coconut oil

  • eggs- when baking, it's good to use room temperature eggs

  • vanilla extract

  • boiling water

Steps

  1. Prep.Preheat the oven to 350ÂºF. Prepare two 9-inch cake pans by spraying them with baking spray or buttering and lightly flouring them.

  2. Whisk dry ingredients. Add flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, salt and espresso powder to a large bowl or the bowl of a stand mixer and whisk.

  3. Mix in wet ingredients. Add milk, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla to flour mixture and mix together on medium speed until well combined. Reduce speed and carefully add boiling water to the cake batter until well combined.

  4. Bake. Distribute cake batter evenly between the two prepared cake pans. Bake for 30-35 minutes, until a toothpick or cake tester inserted in the center of the chocolate cake comes out clean.

  5. Frost. Remove from the oven and allow to cool for about 10 minutes, remove from the pan and cool completely. Frost with Chocolate Buttercream Frosting.
