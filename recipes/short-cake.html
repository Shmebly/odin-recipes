Strawberry Shortcake

Description

This is by far the easiest recipe you can do to make a delicious Strawberry Shortcake! This recipe serves 6 people and can be done within 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 quart fresh strawberries, sliced

  • Â¼ cup white sugar or to taste

  • 1 (12 ounce) package prepared sponge cake dessert cups

  • 1 (7 ounce) can whipped cream, or to taste

Directions

  1. Place strawberries in a bowl; add sugar and stir to coat. Cover and refrigerate until sugar has dissolved, about 15 minutes.

  2. Place 1 dessert cup in each serving bowl and smother with sweetened strawberries. Top each with whipped cream and enjoy.
