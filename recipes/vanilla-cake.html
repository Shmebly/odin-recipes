Vanilla Cake

Description

This wonderful and easy to make vanilla cake is sure to make it to one of your regular recipes. Anyone can recreate this delicious cake to share with your friends and family! You can even add on to this simple cake such as blueberries or even raspberry jello.

Ingredients

For this cake you'll need the following ingredients.

  • cooking spray

  • 2 ⅔ cups all-purpose flour, or more as needed

  • 1 cup white sugar

  • 1 tablespoon baking powder

  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

  • 2 pinches salt

  • 3 eggs

  • ¾ cup milk

  • ¾ cup vegetable oil
